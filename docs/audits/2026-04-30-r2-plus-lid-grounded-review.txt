**Q1 Direction**

Approach C plus the LanguageDetector split is the right structural direction, but the plan needs revisions before it is implementable as written.

On Phase 0 determinism: the LID window construction itself is deterministic. `runMultiWindowLID` derives fixed ranges from `LanguageDetectorThresholds.windows`, dedupes them, caps to four windows, and calls `detectLangauge` sequentially at `LanguageDetector.swift:525-571`. There is no local clock, seed, or parallel reduction in that loop. However, the classifier around it is stateful and time-sensitive unless the test harness injects a fixed clock and isolated defaults: `detect` reads `clock.now()`, prunes memory, and filters recent accepts at `LanguageDetector.swift:136-140`; session prior reads affect decisions at `LanguageDetector.swift:232-236`; flip-flop history is updated at `LanguageDetector.swift:645-651`. There is also a tie-order risk: ranking is over `multi.voteCounts.keys` with vote/mean-prob tie breakers only at `LanguageDetector.swift:201-206`, so exact equal ties can inherit dictionary key-order instability. The tolerance is not the fix for that; the harness should seed memory/clock and add deterministic tie breaking.

The worker is less deterministic than the plan implies. The live incremental loop sleeps on wall-clock cadence and samples whatever the provider returns after OS scheduling at `WhisperKitIncrementalWorker.swift:207-212`; decode timing is explicitly wall-clock via `CFAbsoluteTimeGetCurrent()` at `WhisperKitIncrementalWorker.swift:215` and accumulated at `WhisperKitIncrementalWorker.swift:253-255`. Tail timing is also wall-clock at `WhisperKitIncrementalWorker.swift:131-150` and `WhisperKitIncrementalWorker.swift:186-191`. Transcripts may still be stable for a fixed offline provider, but decode counts, coverage, and timings are not safely byte-for-byte unless the test bypasses or controls cadence.

The proposed `WhisperKitIncrementalSession` method surface matches pipeline behavior: pipeline cancels at `WhisperKitPipeline.swift:244`, `WhisperKitPipeline.swift:536`, and `WhisperKitPipeline.swift:1089`; finalizes at `WhisperKitPipeline.swift:765-768`; starts the worker at `WhisperKitPipeline.swift:1145-1156`.
