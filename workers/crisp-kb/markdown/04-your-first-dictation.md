### Quick start

1. **Launch EnviousWispr.** The lip icon appears in your menu bar.
2. **Complete onboarding.** The guided setup walks you through choosing a speech engine, setting your hotkey, and granting permissions.
3. **Open any app** where you want to type (email, Slack, a document, etc.).
4. **Hold your hotkey** and speak naturally. A floating recording overlay appears to confirm you are recording.
5. **Release the hotkey.** EnviousWispr transcribes your speech, optionally polishes it, and pastes the result directly into the active text field.

### What to expect

* The menu bar icon animates to show recording (rainbow bars), processing (rotating wheel), and completion states.
* A floating overlay gives visual feedback during recording without stealing focus from your app.
* Text is pasted automatically. Your clipboard contents are preserved and restored after pasting.
* If AI polish is enabled, filler words like "um" and "uh" are removed and grammar is cleaned up.

### Tips

* Speak at your normal pace. No need to slow down or enunciate artificially.
* EnviousWispr captures a 500ms pre-roll buffer, so it picks up the beginning of your speech even if you start talking slightly before the hotkey registers.
* You can review past dictations in the History tab of the settings window.
