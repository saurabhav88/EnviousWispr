How It Works

Four steps.
Under two seconds.

A streaming pipeline that starts transcribing while you're still talking — no waiting after you stop.

Parakeet v3 on Apple Neural Engine at 110× speed. Streams as you speak.

Polish

LLM removes fillers, fixes grammar. Works with OpenAI, Gemini, Ollama, or Apple Intelligence.

Paste

Polished text auto-pastes into any app. Previous clipboard preserved.
