Push-to-Talk (PTT) is the default recording mode. Hold your hotkey to record, release it to stop and transcribe.

### How it works

1. Press and hold your designated hotkey.
2. Speak naturally while holding the key.
3. Release the key when you are done speaking.
4. EnviousWispr transcribes your speech, applies any enabled processing (filler removal, custom words, AI polish), and pastes the result.

### Pre-warm on key-down

When you press the hotkey, EnviousWispr immediately begins pre-warming the audio input. If you are using a Bluetooth device, this triggers the codec switch so the microphone is ready by the time you start speaking. This keeps perceived latency sub-second.

### Hands-free extension

While in Push-to-Talk mode, you can **double-press** the hotkey to lock the recording into hands-free mode. This lets you release the key and continue speaking for longer dictations. See the *Hands-Free Mode* article for details.

### Setting

Open the settings window, go to the **Shortcuts** tab, and select **Push-to-Talk** as the recording mode.
