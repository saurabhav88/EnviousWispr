Apple Intelligence provides free, on-device AI polish with no API key required. Your text never leaves your Mac.

### Requirements

* macOS 26 or later.
* A Mac with Apple Intelligence support (Apple Silicon).
* Apple Intelligence must be enabled in System Settings.

### Setup

1. Ensure Apple Intelligence is enabled in **System Settings** > **Apple Intelligence & Siri**.
2. In EnviousWispr, open settings and go to the **AI Polish** tab.
3. Select **Apple Intelligence** as the provider.

### Diagnostics

If Apple Intelligence is unavailable, EnviousWispr runs a five-stage diagnostic check that explains why. Common reasons:

* macOS version too old (needs macOS 26+).
* Apple Intelligence not enabled in System Settings.
* Language model not yet downloaded or not ready.
* Hardware does not meet Apple Intelligence requirements.

The diagnostics results are shown in the AI Polish settings tab when Apple Intelligence is selected.
