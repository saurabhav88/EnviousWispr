Benefits

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for people who think faster than they type.

Private by Default

Your voice is processed entirely on your Mac. Nothing is uploaded. Nothing is stored. Not even by us. Speech recognition runs on Apple Silicon, not in the cloud.

Works Everywhere

Dictate into any app — email, Slack, docs, notes, terminals. Text appears right where you're typing. Your previous clipboard is preserved.

Cleans Up Your Words

Removes ums, fixes grammar, and polishes your text automatically. Choose from clean, formal, or casual styles — or write your own custom prompt.

Blazing Fast

Start talking, stop talking, text appears. The whole process takes about 2 seconds from the moment you release your hotkey.

Your AI, Your Choice

Works with OpenAI, Google Gemini, Apple Intelligence, or fully offline with Ollama. Bring your own API key — or go completely local, no internet required.

Knows Your Words

Add names, technical terms, and company jargon to your personal dictionary. It learns how you talk and gets your words right every time.

Privacy

Your voice stays
on your Mac.

On-device transcription means the audio never leaves your hardware. No accounts. No data collection. No surprises.

On-device processing

Speech recognition runs entirely on your Mac. Your audio is processed in memory and never written to disk or sent anywhere.

No data collection

We don't collect usage data, transcripts, or any personal information. There's no account to create and nothing to opt out of.

Optional cloud polish — you choose

The only optional network call is AI text polishing. You control which provider you use, or you can use Ollama to stay fully offline.
