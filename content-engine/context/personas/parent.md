# The Busy Parent

## Who they are
Parents juggling childcare, household management, and work — often simultaneously. Their hands are rarely free and their attention is constantly divided.

## Pain points
- Can't type when holding a baby, making lunch, or supervising homework
- Constantly interrupted mid-sentence, losing track of what they were writing
- Limited desk time means emails and messages pile up throughout the day
- Existing voice tools send audio to the cloud, which feels wrong when kids are in the background

## How they'd use EnviousWispr
- Dictate email replies and Slack messages while holding a toddler or folding laundry
- Capture grocery lists, to-do items, and appointment notes hands-free throughout the day
- Reply to school messages and teacher emails quickly between tasks without sitting down at a desk
- Use the privacy toggle to pause processing during sensitive family conversations

## Keywords they search for
- "hands free typing mac"
- "voice to text while multitasking"
- "dictation app that works offline mac"
- "best speech to text for busy parents"
- "type without keyboard macOS"

## Tone shift
Warm, empathetic, zero jargon. Acknowledge the chaos without being condescending. Focus on practical time savings and the relief of getting things done without needing both hands free. Never assume they have time to configure anything complicated — emphasize that it just works.
