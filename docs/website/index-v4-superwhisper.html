Process

Four steps, zero friction.

The whole pipeline runs locally on your Mac, invisible and instant.

01

Record

Hold your hotkey and speak naturally. EnviousWispr captures your audio in real time with near-zero latency.

02

Transcribe

WhisperKit converts your speech to text on-device. No internet needed — your voice never leaves your Mac.

03

Polish

An LLM cleans up filler words, fixes grammar, and applies your chosen tone — Clean, Formal, or Casual.

04

Paste

The polished text lands exactly where your cursor is. No clipboard juggling, no app switching required.
