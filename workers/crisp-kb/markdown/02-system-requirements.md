### Minimum requirements

* **macOS:** macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later.
* **Chip:** Apple Silicon (M1 or later). Intel Macs are not supported.
* **Microphone:** Built-in microphone or any external mic. AirPods and Bluetooth headsets are supported with automatic audio routing.

### Recommended

* For Apple Intelligence polish: macOS 26 or later on a Mac with Apple Intelligence support.
* For Ollama local AI polish: enough RAM to run your chosen local model alongside EnviousWispr.

### Disk space

The app itself is lightweight. Speech recognition models are downloaded on first launch:

* **Parakeet v3:** Downloaded automatically during onboarding.
* **WhisperKit:** Models are downloaded when you select this engine. The default model is large-v3-turbo.
