How EnviousWispr Works

Four steps. Under two seconds.

Step 01
🎙
Record

Press your hotkey and talk naturally. No need to slow down or speak formally — just say what you mean.

Step 02
⚡
Transcribe

AI on your Mac converts speech to text instantly. Everything runs locally — your voice never leaves your device.

Step 03
✨
Polish

Grammar is fixed, filler words are removed, and your meaning is preserved — automatically, in the tone you choose.

Step 04
📋
Paste

Clean, polished text appears wherever you're typing — any app, any field, without switching windows.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

🧠
On-device transcription

Powered by WhisperKit and Apple Speech, transcription runs entirely on your Mac. No audio ever leaves your device.

✏️
Smart polish

Filler words removed, grammar corrected, your meaning preserved. Choose your tone: clean, formal, or casual.

🎯
Voice activity detection

Automatically detects when you start and stop speaking, so you never have to babysit the recording process.

🖥
Works in any app

Polished text pastes into any macOS application — email, Slack, Notes, browser forms, terminals — everything.

🔒
Push-to-talk and hands-free

Hold your hotkey to record, or double-press to lock into hands-free mode for longer dictation sessions.

🔌
Multiple AI providers

Connect to OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, or a local LLM. Bring your own API key — no subscription required.

🔤
Custom word correction

Teach the app to recognize names, acronyms, and domain-specific terms that general models consistently get wrong.

🍎
Native macOS app

Built in Swift for macOS 14 and up. Lives in your menu bar, uses minimal resources, feels at home on your Mac.

⚙️
Streaming pipeline

Recording, transcription, and AI polishing run in parallel — the result arrives almost the moment you stop speaking.
