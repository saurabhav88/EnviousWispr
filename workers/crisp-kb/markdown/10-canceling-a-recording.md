You can cancel an in-progress recording at any time. The audio is discarded and nothing is transcribed or pasted.

### Methods to cancel

* **Escape key:** Press Escape during any recording to cancel immediately. This is the default cancel hotkey (key code 53, no modifiers).
* **Triple-press (Hands-Free mode):** If you are in a hands-free locked recording, triple-press the hotkey to cancel.

### What happens on cancel

* Recording stops immediately.
* All captured audio is discarded.
* No transcription runs, no text is pasted.
* The pipeline returns to idle state.
