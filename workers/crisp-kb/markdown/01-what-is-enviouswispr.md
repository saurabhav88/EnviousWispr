EnviousWispr is a macOS dictation app that turns your speech into polished, paste-ready text. The entire pipeline runs on your Mac using Apple Silicon. Your audio never leaves your device.

### How it works

1. **Record:** Press your hotkey and speak naturally.
2. **Transcribe:** On-device speech recognition converts your voice to text in real time.
3. **Polish:** Optional AI polish fixes grammar, removes filler words, and preserves your meaning.
4. **Paste:** Clean text appears in whatever app you are typing in. No window switching required.

### Key facts

* 100% free. No subscription, no account required.
* Two speech engines: Parakeet v3 (English, streaming) and WhisperKit (multi-language).
* Five AI polish providers: Apple Intelligence, Ollama, OpenAI, Gemini, or None.
* Works in any app that accepts text input: Slack, VS Code, Gmail, Notion, Google Docs, Terminal, and more.
* Menu bar app that stays out of your way. No dock icon.
