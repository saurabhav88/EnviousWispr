Re-polish takes the original ASR text from a saved transcript and runs it through the text processing chain again with your current settings. This lets you:

* Try a different LLM provider (switch from OpenAI to Gemini, or from Ollama to Apple Intelligence)
* Change the writing style (Standard, Formal, Friendly, or Custom)
* Apply a different custom system prompt
* Test the effect of enabling or disabling filler word removal

## How to Re-Polish

Open **History** in the settings window, select a transcript, and use the re-polish action. The transcript is reprocessed using whatever AI Polish settings are currently configured. The original ASR text is always preserved.

## No Re-Recording Needed

Re-polish works entirely from the saved ASR text. You do not need to speak again. This is useful when you change your AI settings and want to see how they affect past dictations.
