Toggle mode is an alternative to Push-to-Talk. Instead of holding a key, you press once to start and once to stop.

### How it works

1. Press your hotkey once. Recording starts.
2. Speak naturally.
3. Press the hotkey again. Recording stops and transcription begins.

### When to use Toggle mode

* When you prefer not to hold down a key while speaking.
* For medium-length dictations where you know when you want to stop.

### Differences from Push-to-Talk

* Hands-free double-press locking is a Push-to-Talk feature. In Toggle mode, recording is already hands-free by default.
* The hotkey label in the menu bar shows the key name without "Hold" prefix.

### Setting

Open the settings window, go to the **Shortcuts** tab, and select **Toggle** as the recording mode.
